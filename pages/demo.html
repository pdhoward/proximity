LIVE DEMO

FAST

You compose and publish messages quickly. Venue owners curate content, to ensure safe and appropriate messages. Pages are statically generated and delivered from the ‘edge of the internet’ right to a shopper’s smart phone.

POINT OF DECISION

You reach shoppers further along in the purchasing process -- right at the point of decision. Deliver an offer, information, or insights to help promote your Brand or product just when it counts! And convert the shopper to a buyer!

SECURE

Your Brand message is secure. All pages are statically generated and hosted at the 'edge of the internet'. Our secure publishing cycle ensures that your messages are available on time, in the venues you choose and with the security you expect.

ANALYTICS

Our dashboards are a game changer for your marketing team, giving you insights into the effectiveness of your campaigns through time, location, and event based analytics. Additional context, such as demographics, community events and traffic data help to enrich the information for even more compelling insights.

CURATED

The messages delivered by Brands have been carefully curated by store managers, ensuring that shoppers at their venue receive the ads and messages most meaningful to them. For Brands, this eliminates the risk of adverse content and ad placement.

SEGMENTATION

Our message platform delivers a unique intersection of venue locations, demographics and community events permitting Brands to choose profiles which best meet their campaign objectives. Segmentation is built in.

STORES NEARBY

Shoppers can choose stores right from their smartphone app, using location-based search. The app provides shoppers with a variety of options to locate their store, or even discover new venues through the 'stores nearby' button.

RICH MEDIA

Brands have a wide range of cool options for enhancing their messages and ads with rich media. The platform makes it easy to deliver video, audio, animation, images, and text. Microapps are coming soon, with options for payments, orders, shipments, and chat.
